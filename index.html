The easiest way to get into the metaverse.

All you need is a smartphone. No VR goggles, no wallets, no cryptography. We generate you a private key (and put you on the blockchain) algorithmically from your username and password.

Take back control of your data.

Other social medias have full control over your personal information. In the Eraverse, we are different. Only you have access to your private key (we never even see it) and we use the blockchain to build privacy, security, and transparency into our tech itself.
